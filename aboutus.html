Welcome to the Alumni portal of ABC University.
ABC university is the most prestigious university of South India, located in the beautiful city of Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh. It is India's 15th ranked Engineering University known for its unique teaching methods and infrastructure.

About the website

This is a website maintained by ABC University. It is an alumni feedback system.
- Alumni of ABC university help the institution to improve by filling a feedback form. The feedback given by our alumni helps the institution and our faculty to improve and make the institution a better place. The suggestions and complains by the alumni are taken positively and used for the betterment of the university and its surroundings.
- The Alumni can also help our students by posting details about any existing internship opportunities. This can help our students to find internships and good jobs.
- The website will also update important news to keep the alumni updated. - Alumni can share their experiences with our students.
- The details of the Alumni can also be searched in the portal.
